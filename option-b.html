Infrastructure

8,000 sq ft, Bharuch, Gujarat.

A climate-controlled, dust-free plant designed to international hygiene standards, in India's GIDC industrial corridor about 350 km from Mumbai.

Machinery

8-Colour Rotogravure Printing

High-speed with auto registration and web guiding, for precise multi-colour print across long production runs.

Solvent-Less Lamination

High-speed lamination for eco-friendly, food-safe multi-layer structures with excellent bond strength.

Solvent-Based Lamination

High-speed lamination for specialised barrier structures requiring solvent adhesive systems.

Slitting Machine

Precise winding tension control for accurate finishing of film, laminates, and paper to spec.

Pouching Machines

Dedicated machines for Centre Seal and Side Seal pouches / bags across a wide range of sizes.

Facility at a glance
