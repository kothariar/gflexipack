Welcome to our company

Quality packaging, since 2016.

G Flexipack is a flexible packaging manufacturer in Bharuch, Gujarat, built on the Gangamani group's 20+ years of manufacturing heritage. We print, laminate, slit and convert custom pouches for brands across food, pharma, personal care and industry — in a climate-controlled, dust-free plant designed to international hygiene standards.
