Stand-Up Pouches.

A stand-up pouch is a versatile packaging solution designed to remain upright on flat surfaces thanks to its bottom gusset. Sealed sides protect the product and prevent leakage, an optional zipper adds reusability, and a choice of finishes and thicknesses tailors the pouch to your product and your shelf.
